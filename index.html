  • Why

    Have you searched for a spiritual community where you belong and are known, only to find yourself hurt, intimidated or rejected by organized religion?

    We understand how you feel. In fact, we have met many people who felt the same way until they joined us around The Table. These folks found something different at The Table -- a welcoming community where everyone's story matters and where everyone belongs.

    At The Table we believe that a thoughtfully engaged spiritual community should be the norm, not the exception. Our conversations are invigorating and accessible, and they lead to creative action.

    Transforming the world begins with us. We join forces for good in Casper and beyond with anyone who is willing to love alongside of us. At The Table we are helping to create a better community and a better world through love and compassion for all our neighbors and planet.

  • Who

    You! That's Who!

    There is always room for one more around this table.

    Don't go through life feeling unknown and rejected.
    Come tell your story and belong to a community that embraces all.

    Don't isolate yourself from a community where your questions matter.
    Come share your beliefs, joys and successes, as well as your doubts, fears and failures.

    Don't miss out on the chance to live a life that really makes a difference.
    Come join forces with others who want to see love win locally & globally.

  • What

    We love good coffee & other warm beverages
    We think shared meals are the best meals
    We share a meal together every Wednesday at 5:30pm helping to meet two really basic human needs: nutrition & friendship
    (There are always vegetarian friendly options)
    We share common practices every time we get together listening, hospitality, compassion & action.
    We’re experimental
    >> sometimes we dialogue, sometimes we listen,
    >> sometimes we serve, sometimes we help others out,
    >> sometimes we engage with art, sometimes we listen to poetry,
    >> sometimes we pray, sometimes we study,
    >> sometimes we meditate, sometimes we sing.
