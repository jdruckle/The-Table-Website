"Documentary Featuring The Table & Red Clay Abbey"

Posted on November 19, 2022 by Libby Hugus

Did you know The Table & the Red Clay Abbey are supported by an umbrella organization?

1001 New Worshiping Communities was the fertile soil in which we were planted to grow and blossom...

This gorgeous & informative little documentary tells the story of this 10 year old movement. The Table has participated since 2014, Just 18 months after the movement was organized in 2012. Check it out... you may see some familiar faces. We're SO VERY PROUD that The Red Clay Abbey & Jessika Waldron are featured in this narrative. Also visible: Pastor Libby, Coach Bj Woodworth, colleagues Shayla Graham & Steve Shive.

WOW! Thanks be to God & the Universal Christ!
