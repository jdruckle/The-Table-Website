yoga on the labyrinth & solabration


Solabration on the Circle is Casper’s Annual Summer Solar Festival.


We kicked off this tradition with a bang in 2017 when we found ourselves on the centerline of the Total Eclipse. Partnering with the Wyoming Eclipse Festival, The Table Casper and Beyond Borders Adventure Retreats hosted a sold-out crowd of humans from all over the world to celebrate the wonders our universe, art, music, and gathering in unity and peace.

In 2018 we hosted the World Champion Apache Hoop Dancers, Yellow Bird Productions, for an incredible afternoon of art, celebration, dance and spectacle.

In 2019, we featured international bestseller and storyteller don Jose Ruiz, Miguel Nardi: mantra meditation expert from Oregon, Red Butte Band from Casper, WY; Banshee Tree of Boulder, CO, Casper Kirtan, as well as high energy Yoga & Qigong, plus Sacred Sounds.

Every dollar raised for this event is reinvested neighbor to neighbor through our non-profit, community action arm of The Table, a 501(c)3.

Solabration 2022 is Casper’s sixth annual Sunfest. Join us for a free wellness and multicultural gathering with engaging performances, live music, and peaceful community. We are scheduling around Funky Junk so you can enjoy both and International Day of Peace and the Fall Equinox, the Tour de Platte Peace Parade, and quality entertainment. Scan the Schedule QR Code on the poster for times.
