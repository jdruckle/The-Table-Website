Our Partners

The Table is a catalyst for the greater good of Casper and beyond


Our partners join us by investing, neighbor-to-neighbor, their financial investment and volunteer support in the important work of making our neighborhood a compassionate and welcoming place to call home and a community where all belong.

We are a community of action, and regularly step into our neighborhood to support those in addiction recovery to practice spiritually intuitive presence for strengthening mental health; amplifying the marginalized voices in our community like the Native, Black, Latinx, poor and incarcerated; we lift up local artists; our downtown business associates; and advocate for immigrants and refugees wrestling through the immigration system. We want to spark peace and justice through our action for the common good.
